About me

Hi! I'm Kevin. I'm an AI Research Engineer working for TCS Research, India. In 2024, I graduated from Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology, Shibpur with a bachelors in Computer Science.

My research focuses on AI alignment and preference modelling i.e. designing methods that allow models to learn human judgement, taste and intent rather than imitate surface patterns. I'm particularly interested in distilling human novelty, creativity and preferences into learning systems. My work involves working across both text and image modalities, studying how these signals can be represented and measured.

For a Layman: I teach machines how to be more human :)
